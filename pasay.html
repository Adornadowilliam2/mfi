    MFI PASAY CAMPUS

    Another campus of MFI located in Southern Metro Manila, MFI Pasay is located at 2240 MFI Building, Taft Avenue, Pasay City, In-between Gil Puyat and LRT Libertad Station beside BDO Bank. Just like the Pasig Campus, the Pasay Branch caters to companies that want to have customized courses tailored around their needs and expectations. Short courses from various technologies are conducted here for those who want to have it at the campus itself. Most of the clients of the Pasay Branch are from Southern Metro Manila and Southern Luzon, particularly the CALABARZON area.
