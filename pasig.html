    MFI PASIG CAMPUS

    The main campus of MFI is the Pasig Campus, located in the Ortigas District of Pasig City and proximate to The Medical City hospital.

    The Pasig campus caters to students of senior high school and dual training system (DTS) programs. Short courses from various technologies are conducted here for those who want to have it at the campus itself. Customized training programs are also available at the Pasig campus. The campus houses the different departments and business units

    Electronics Lab

    The Electronics Lab at MFI is equipped with the latest tools and technology to provide hands-on learning experiences. Students have access to advanced circuit boards, soldering stations, and a wide range of electronic components. This facility is designed for students to develop skills in electronics, electrical engineering, and innovative circuit design, preparing them for both academic and industry success.

    Short Course Training Center

    The Short Course Training Center at MFI is dedicated to providing specialized training programs in various technologies and fields. From IT certifications to hands-on workshops in digital marketing and graphic design, this facility caters to individuals looking to boost their skills and advance their careers. The center is equipped with state-of-the-art computers, software, and dedicated spaces for practical training and group activities.
